As a node for urban micro-environment and road condition mapping, motion sensors record road vibrations (such as potholes and speed bumps), while ENV sensors create temperature/humidity microclimate maps of different neighborhoods. I'm quite interested in this idea. I think it's possible to design a program that cyclists can download and use to record areas of poor road conditions in the city, then record them on a map, simultaneously creating temperature/humidity microclimate maps of different neighborhoods, uploading them to the cloud, and alerting other cyclists to potential hazards.
